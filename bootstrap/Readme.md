basic grid 

the space is divided into 12 columns 
you can use span{number} and get that many columns 

eg .
<!--bootstrap-->
<div class="container">
    <div class="row">
      <div class="span4">Span 4</div>
      <div class="span8">Span 8</div>
    </div>
</div>

here you will occupy 4 columns and 8 columns
